Welcome to St Alphonsus R.C. Primary School.

Welcome to St. Alphonsus R.C. Primary School, where learning is a joy and our committed team works to provide a loving, Christian environment where each person is valued equally. We value the privilege of working together with parents on all aspects of their child's education and development, establishing a cordial alliance that benefits our kids' general development.

History

We at St. Alphonsus R.C. Primary School have high expectations for every student, and we provide a varied and stimulating curriculum that prioritises fun and inquiry. We consider the establishment of a safe and secure environment to be the reason why our school is a happy place. Here, kids grow confidently because we offer the perfect, caring environment for their development and educational journey.
